Mesaieed International School

Naslat Al Galayel Street, PO Box 50070, Mesaieed, Qatar.

There are three campuses. These are the EYFS campus, primary campus and secondary campus.

My school campus.

1100-1200 students between the ages of 3 to 19 years attend this school.
Mesaieed International School (MIS) primarily follows the National Curriculum for England.
The school offers a broad range of subjects including core subjects like English, Mathematics, and Arabic (for native speakers),
alongside foundation subjects like Science, History, Geography, Art, Design & Technology, Music, Computing, and Physical Education.
For secondary students, the school offers subjects leading to IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education) and A-Level qualifications, preparing students for university or further education.
They also have specific programs like STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) and competitions like the Programming and Artificial Intelligence Olympics.
Mesaieed International School is accredited by Edexcel, Cambridge Assessment International Education, and BTEC.
